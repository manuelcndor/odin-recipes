Lomo Saltado

Description

Lomo saltado is a beloved Peruvian stir-fry that beautifully blends Chinese and Peruvian culinary traditions. It features tender strips of beef sautéed over high heat with red onions, tomatoes, and ají amarillo (yellow chili pepper), then tossed with soy sauce, vinegar, and fresh cilantro. The quick cooking method locks in bold flavors and keeps the vegetables slightly crisp.

It’s traditionally served with both rice and crispy French fries—a delicious fusion that reflects Peru’s multicultural history. Lomo saltado is hearty, savory, and satisfying, with a smoky edge from the wok. It’s a staple in Peruvian households.
